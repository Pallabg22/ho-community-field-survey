Nagara / Dhamsha

Nagara or Dhamsha is a large kettle drum used by the Ho community during festivals, rituals, and important social occasions.

Madal

Madal is a traditional double-headed drum made of clay or wood. It plays an important role in Ho folk music and dance performances.

Ghungur

Ghungur consists of small bells worn around the ankles, especially during traditional dances, adding rhythm to movement and music.

Rut-tu

Rut-tu is a traditional bamboo flute used for melodic expression in Ho cultural and social gatherings.
